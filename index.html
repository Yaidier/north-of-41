The goal of ‘North of 41′ is to promote the tech industry and all of the cutting edge innovation that is taking place from coast to coast.

Our [Training] Programm

As part of our network, we offer some of the most unique training programs in the tech industry today. We offer everything from learning how to code, to learning how to enhance your personal brand from media relation experts.

Our team of experts will help customize a program to fit your needs. What better way to learn then from those stakeholders who are leaders in the tech industry. Our programs can be delivered both in person and online which provides groups and organizations with the ultimate flexibility.
